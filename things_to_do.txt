  🔧 Medium-effort, high-payoff

  Performance
  - dispatch runs the reducer twice per action (editor-context.tsx:3547-3574) — once for stateRef mirror, once for React. With mark-document-dirty
   follow-ups, up to 4× per high-frequency action. Consider useSyncExternalStore to unify.
  - blurStamp/sharpenStamp allocate a full Uint8ClampedArray copy + double-pass per dab (tool-helpers.ts:1693, :1764) — fold the circle test into
  the first pass, write back in-place.
  - lensBlur (filters.ts:2086) and radialBlur (filters.ts:2204) not in WORKER_SUPPORTED_FILTERS — main-thread block. surfaceBlur (filters.ts:2166)
   is O(w·h·r²) without tiling — multi-second pause on 4K.
  - menu-bar.tsx:20 eagerly imports the full 170KB FILTERS registry just to render submenu names. Split into filters-meta.ts (id/name/category)
  and filters.ts (kernels); await import() kernels on invocation.
  - Filter algorithms duplicated between filters.ts and the worker source string (filter-worker.ts:191+ has copies of boxBlur, brightnessContrast,
   etc.) — extract a shared no-DOM kernel module the worker can importScripts.

  Architecture
  - Layer is a fat optional bag (types.ts:770-819) with text?, shape?, path?, adjustment?, etc. all optional — drives most of the as unknown as
  and ! calls. Split into a discriminated union on kind. (Large change but pays off everywhere.)
  - Field-by-field layer-clone ladder duplicated in 4 places (editor-context.tsx:600-629, :632-663, :2580-2606, :2920-2998) — every new optional
  field breaks one of duplicate/history/comp until you patch all four. Extract cloneLayerFields.
  - commit() wrapped in setTimeout(0) at 145 call sites across 33 files (e.g. advanced-subsystems-dialog.tsx:903/1440/1448/1458) — comment at
  editor-context.tsx:3801 says the defer isn't needed. Race window for input on the next tick.
  - 102 places call layer.canvas.getContext().drawImage(...) then dispatch later — state mutation outside the reducer with React unaware. Add a
  mutate-layer-canvas action that runs draw + history snapshot atomically.
  - AdvancedSubsystemsDialog is 1796 lines, eagerly imports 3D/video/color-pipeline engines (advanced-subsystems-dialog.tsx) — split workspaces
  into per-tab lazy chunks.

  Accessibility

  - Command palette <Input> lacks role="combobox", aria-expanded, aria-controls, aria-activedescendant (command-palette.tsx:771-803) — highlighted
   commands aren't announced.
  - ImageSizeDialog and similar (image-size-dialog.tsx:80-127) — <Label> without htmlFor, bare <select> unlabeled. Pattern repeats across
